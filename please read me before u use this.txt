

GoAnimate 2016 Video Player Page

-------------------------------------------

This page will make it look like GoAnimate style of it
if you click the white and gray (thumbnail) next to the player
the text will pop up, and when you click "Hide" the text will 
go away, pretty cool huh?

- How do you get this in Wrapper Offline though? if you asked

Well there is a way!

First, extract the zip file

then, drag the "Mugen Yujo - S1 E1 - To Be Dared - Mugen Yujo_files" folder into the server folder
just like so!

Next, put the "player.eta" on wrapper/views/app/, drag it there.

Now, you need to put the app.js in /wrapper/controllers files in it!

Good. now restart Wrapper offline

Click any video that you've made, or make a new video

And BAM! you got the GoAnimate 2016 Video Payer Page!
